Welcome to Teens for Alzheimers!
This website is intended to be a resource for teens that are passionate about finding a cure and want to do anything to help. Our generation has to work to find a cure so we can prevent it from affecting anybody else, including us.
Being educated about the disease is crucial to finding a cure more quickly and efficiently.


There are four approaches to doing so:
-edcuating yourself and others
-appreciating and understanding those living with the disease
-advocating for a cure
-donating or raising funds to go to research

What is Alzheimer's?

Alzheimer’s disease is a progressive brain disease that has symptoms that slowly start to interfere with memory.

-Alzheimer’s is the most common type of dementia, and usually occurs in those age 65 or older.
-But, there is also early-onset Alzheimer’s that occurs in approximately 200,000 Americans.
-The disease progresses from mild to moderate to severe, as it is a terminal illness.
